  • Ramen
    Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup dish made with wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, usually topped with ingredients like sliced pork, eggs, and vegetables. 🍜
  • Udon
    Udon is a Japanese dish made of thick wheat noodles served in a hot broth, often topped with ingredients like green onions, tempura, or tofu. 🍜
  • Mochi
    Mochi is a Japanese rice cake made from glutinous rice that is pounded into a soft, chewy texture and often filled with sweet or savory ingredients. 🍡
